Become A Member

Why?

  1. Plant and harvest your own healthy vegetables, fruits, berries & mushrooms.
  2. Create healthy ecosystems - For people, animals and nature.
  3. Learn the basics of permaculture.
  4. Celebrate with us and get to know exciting people!

How?

Write an email to seedcity@ethz.ch to coordinate an initial meeting. After we get to know you in the garden, you will be warmly welcomed into the Telegram chat. The chat is our official internal communication channel.

If you had the possibility to get know the garden and you like the concept, consider becoming a member by supporting us with a financial contribution of your choice. Please send an email to seedcity@ethz.ch.
