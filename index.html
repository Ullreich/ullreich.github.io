Our Mission

We are an open community, consisting of both ETH students, as well as people from the Höngg neighborhood. In our community garden, we build on shared responsibility and self-initiative. All members have the opportunity to get involved and unfold their interests - be it in the organization or in garden design. Together, we maintain all beds and garden elements and celebrate bountiful and healthy harvests. We share both the work and the harvest.

Our Beginning

Since 2010, an oasis of biodiversity has emerged on approximately 2100 m², inviting visitors to teach and learn, to get involved and relax, to celebrate, contemplate, and enjoy moments of peace.

Get involved

News and general meetups are published in our Telegram chat. Once we have met you in the garden, you will be warmly welcomed into the chat as well. Write an email to seedcity@ethz.ch for the coordination of a first meeting.

Be a part of us and contribute your ideas: Just visit us and get inspired. If you're lucky, there will be pizza :)
